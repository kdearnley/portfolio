About Me

Ever since highschool I have been making my own websites mostly for fun. It wasn't until the pandemic when my job in travel fell apart that I returned to web development and fell in love with it all over again. I love the challenge of making information clear and user interfaces easy while constantly learning something new in the forever changing landscape of technology. Plus the added bonus of making a really good webpage is always exciting!

Projects

Visit Ararat

The Problem: While visiting Ararat I was surprised at how much it had to offer for a country town, but its web presence was lacking. All tourist info was an afterthought on the city council website with menus being different on every page which made navigation a nightmare.

The Solution: Using React I have created a new website highlighting the museums and local restaurants making browsing easy and finding the information you’re looking for a breeze while also looking good and up-to-date.

Journey Builder

The Problem: In my spare time I love researching possible travel plans but wanted something a bit easier and more appealing than an excel spreadsheet to keep track of my research.

The Solution: With this Journey Builder users are able to create itineraries easily while being able to add, edit and delete entries. Built with React, React Hooks and Bootstrap.
